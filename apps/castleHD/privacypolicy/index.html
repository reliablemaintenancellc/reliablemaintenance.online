2. Information We Collect

The App and its integrated third-party services may collect certain information to provide functionality, display advertisements, and improve performance. The categories of information collected may include:

Device and App Information

We may automatically collect technical information about your device and app usage, including:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • App version and installation source
  • Unique device identifiers (such as Android ID)
  • Screen resolution and device language settings
  • Time zone and general usage timestamps

Log Data and Diagnostics

To maintain and improve the App, we may collect diagnostic information, including:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics (app launch time, response times)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Session duration and frequency

Advertising Data

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These services may collect:

  • Google Advertising ID (a resettable identifier for advertising purposes)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, conversions)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country, region, or city level)
  • Information used to serve relevant advertisements based on your interests, where consent is provided

User-Provided Information

The App does not require account registration or login. We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number through the App's core functionality. However, if you contact us for support, we will receive and store the information you provide in your communication (such as your email address and message content).

Support Communications

If you contact us via email for support, feedback, or inquiries, we collect the content of your message, your email address, and any attachments you include. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and improve our services.

Note: We strive to collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the App. Where uncertainty exists about specific data collection by third-party SDKs, we use careful language and encourage you to review the respective third-party privacy policies.

  • Analytics and Diagnostics: To understand how users interact with the App, monitor performance, and diagnose technical issues.
  • Display Advertisements: To show advertisements within the App and measure ad performance. Where applicable and with your consent, ads may be personalized based on your interests.

5. Advertising and Consent (GDPR/EEA)

The App displays advertisements to support its free operation. Advertisements may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your location, consent choices, and applicable regulations.

Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

  • Personalized Ads: These ads are tailored to your interests based on your activity, demographics, or other signals collected by advertising networks. Personalized ads are served only where you have provided consent or where permitted by law.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: These ads are not based on your past behavior or interests. They may still use contextual information (such as general location or the content you are viewing) but do not rely on personal data profiles.

Consent for Users in the EEA/UK

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom (UK), or other jurisdictions requiring consent for personalized advertising, the App may display a consent dialog when you first use the App or when required. You may choose to consent to or decline personalized advertising. Your choice will be respected by our advertising partners.

Managing Your Advertising Preferences

You can manage your advertising preferences through the following methods:

  • Reset Advertising ID: On your Android device, go to Settings > Google > Ads to reset your Advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization.
  • In-App Consent: Where applicable, you may be able to change your consent preferences within the App or through the consent dialog.
